Our Ethos

Food made from local suppliers.

We believe a great sandwich starts long before the filling. It begins with flour, water, time, and respect. Everything we make — our bread, our coffee, our sandwiches — is crafted with the same attention a baker gives their craft: slowly, thoughtfully, and with genuine care for the person eating it.

"Real food, real people,
real Leeds since 1994."

Our Sandwiches

We specialise in sandwiches. Our range of fillings is vast, and we have a huge selection of vegetarian and vegan fillings.

Our Bread

Our craft is handmade ciabatta and focaccia bread, which we make every night in our bakery.

Our Coffee

We also serve Italian-style coffee, dark-roasted for traditional tastes.

What We Do

More than a sandwich.

Baked In-House

Ciabatta & Focaccia.

Every loaf we use starts in our own kitchen. Our ciabatta is airy, chewy, and perfectly crisped — our focaccia is pillowy, herb-kissed and drizzled with good olive oil. Both are baked fresh each morning so that by the time you're hungry, your bread is at its best.

We don't compromise on the foundation. Because a great sandwich deserves great bread.
